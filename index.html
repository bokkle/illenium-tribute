Here's a timeline of Illenium's life and career

  • 1990 - Nicholas Daniel Miller was born in Downers Grove, Illinois on December 26
  • 2008 - 2012 - Attends the University of Colorado Boulder, where he studies music production and begins experimenting with electronic music
  • 2013 - Releases his debut self-titled EP, marking the start of his music career
  • 2016 - Illenium's second EP, "Ashes," gains significant attention and establishes him as a rising star in the electronic music scene
  • 2017 - Opens up about his past struggles with drug addiction, sharing his personal journey of overcoming challenges and finding inspiration in music
  • 2017 - 2019 - Releases his critically acclaimed albums "Awake" (2017) and "Ascend" (2019), featuring collaborations with various artists and garnering widespread acclaim
  • 2020 - Releases his fourth studio album, "Fallen Embers," showcasing his evolving sound and further solidifying his position as a prominent figure in electronic music
  • 2021 - Embarks on a successful world tour, captivating audiences with his exhilarating live performances and emotional music
  • Present - Illenium continues to innovate and evolve his sound, pushing boundaries in the electronic music genre and captivating fans worldwide with his unique blend of melodic and heartfelt productions
